Cryptocurrency Overview

Cryptocurrencies are digital or virtual currencies that use cryptography for security. They operate on decentralized networks based on blockchain technology. Popular cryptocurrencies include Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Litecoin. The crypto market is known for its volatility and rapid price changes, making it essential for investors to stay informed about market trends and updates.
